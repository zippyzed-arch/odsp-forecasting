Edu Leads Growth (+37%)
Education grew fastest overall — from 23.7M → 37.1M DAU — despite a sharp summer trough. Strong back-to-school rebounds in Sep–Oct each year drive recovery.
☀️
Summer Seasonality Is Universal
Every segment dips Jul–Aug. Enterprise Public Sector drops most severely (−43% from peak to Aug trough). Edu also halves in summer. Commercial is most resilient.
🔀
SME&C Reclassification (Jul 2025)
SME&C Commercial and Public Sector segments appear from July 2025, absorbing the former "Small, Medium & Corporate" categories. SME&C Public Sector nearly doubled since Jul 2025.
🏆
Commercial Dominates at 40% Share
Commercial holds 40% of total DAU as of Mar 2026. Enterprise Commercial and SME&C Commercial together add another 34%, making commercial segments ~74% of usage.
🚀
Mar 2026 is a Record Month
March 2026 shows the highest DAU for all segments — Commercial at 133.9M avg, Enterprise Commercial at 59.0M, and Edu at 32.6M are all all-time highs in this dataset.
📉
Public Sector Lags Commercial Growth
Enterprise Public Sector grew only +10% vs Commercial's +28%. Holiday and fiscal-year sensitivity — Dec and Aug troughs are steeper — may reflect procurement cycles.
